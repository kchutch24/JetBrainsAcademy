Chicken Riggies

Utica style Chicken Riggies dish with added garnish.

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 Chicken Breasts
  • 1lb Riggatoni
  • 1c Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 4-5 Hot Cherry Peppers
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1-2 Onions
  • 1 Stick of Butter
  • 3/4c White Wine (Cooking or Drinking)
  • Black Olives
  • 32oz Marinara Sauce

Directions:

  1. Heat large pan and melt stick of butter
  2. Saute Chicken
  3. When chicken is close to one, simer for 20 minutes and add wine, peppers (green and hot), onions (chopped), garlic powder (to taste).
  4. Boil riggatoni
  5. Add Marinara sauce and heavy cream - do not boil
  6. Simmer 5-10 minutes
  7. Add black olives
  8. Pour over riggatoni
  9. Enjoy!
