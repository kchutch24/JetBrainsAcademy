Grilled cheese sandwhich

Ingredients:

  • Sliced Cheese
  • Butter or Margarine
  • Sliced Bread

Directions:

  1. Heat pan and melt small piece of butter
  2. Butter both sides of two pieces of bread
  3. Place 1-2 slices of cheese on a slice of bread
  4. Close slices of bread together
  5. Add sandwhich to pan
  6. Toast to desired level
  7. Flip sandwhich
  8. Toast other side to desired level
  9. Enjoy!
